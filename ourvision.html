Empower is a newfound company that focusses primarily on the establishment of the careers of hundreds and thousands of poor people in the country of India. We firmly believe that this shall not only encourage thousands of people; but also contribute to the economy of the country in a more holistic picture. We believe in the simple not the complex. In order to focus on the few initiatives that are actually important and meaningful to us, we think it's important to say no to thousands of other ideas. Deep group collaboration and cross-pollination are important to us because they help us innovate in a way that others cannot. We strive to be the most motivating, encouraging, empowering organization out there whilst supporting the major cause of eradicating the problems of hunger and poverty.
