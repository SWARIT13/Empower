Empower

Free yourself from the clutches of poverty! We here at Empower, aim to eradicate poverty and hunger from the world.

Advika was a very wealthy girl. She was running a very successfull company and had everything in life. However, one day a new policy by the government changed her life forever, Her company shot down and she lost everything!! Poverty struck her hard Since then she decided to rebuild her life and the life of the poor people. This is how Empower was born. Since then, Empower has helped over 100,000 artists and has also helped protect the diverse Indian culture.
